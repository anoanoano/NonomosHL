This display can be yours.


Each of the 16 videos on this screen is represented by a unique token on the Ethereum network, numbered 1-16. Token ID 1 is the video in the top left, Token ID 2 is the one below it, and so on (i.e., if you see four columns, the top video in the second column is video 5).

You'll need Metamask to use this site. Enter the token number of the video you want in the Token ID Selector box, and then click the other buttons to do get more info or do something with it.

Click "Acquire Token" to buy it. Don't worry, Metamask will give you a chance to review and approve the transaction.

If you own the selected token, you can change the channel by submitting a new Youtube video ID while the token you own is entered in the "Token ID Selector" box. (If you go look at a Youtube video URL, it ends in v=Blablabla. Blablabla is the video ID.)

If you own a token, you may also pay owed taxes on it, or re-assess it. These assessments and taxations follow a Harberger Taxation system, so that the higher you assess your token, the more it costs someone else to buy it away from you, but the more you have to pay to retain it, and vice-versa.
